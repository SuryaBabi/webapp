Clownfish

A layer of mucus on the clownfish's skin makes it immune to the fish-eating anemone's lethal sing.

  • Amphiprioninae
  • 11 Centimeters
  • 7 Years
  • Tropical Coralreefs

Bright orange with three distinctive white bars,clown anemonefish are among the most recognisable of all reef-dwellers.They reach about 11 centimetres in length and are named after multicoloured sea anemone in which they make their homes.
